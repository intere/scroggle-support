Why free?

There are a ton of reasons to make it free as in beer:
  • There are too many games in the app stores riddled with ads (this drives me nuts)
  • There are too many games in the app store that are paid or subscribe
  • I am an individual, not a company, I don't have a marketing budget
  • I do this for fun, because I like to play the game and I want you to like it too.
There are a ton of reason to make it free as in speech:
  • I can recruit other developers to lend a hand
  • I can ask other developers to review areas of the code without an NDA
  • I can teach other developers who want to make their own games
  • I may get unsolicited critical feedback about how I've built the app
